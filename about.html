Active English Vocabulary

Who speaks English?

English is the second mostly used language in the world with more than 942 million speakers in total.

Active vocabulary

Active vocabulary is vocabulary that you can recall and use at will when the situation requires it. You are choosing to use the word and actively retrieving it from memory.

Is 3000 words enough?

An average adult native English speaker has an active vocabulary of about 20,000 words. However it has been proven that just 3000 words covers around 95% of common texts.

Which words?

We processed and analyzed 15 688 Tweets and 15 564 words to calculate the usage of words and extracted value with statistic tools.

Social networks

The biggest part of Twitter users use English as their first or the second language. We analyzed a great amount of random Tweets to find the most widely used words among people on Internet.
