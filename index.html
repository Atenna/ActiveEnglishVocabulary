English

English is the second mostly used language in the world with more than 942 million speakers in total.

Active vocabulary

Active vocabulary is vocabulary that you can recall and use at will when the situation requires it. You are choosing to use the word and actively retrieving it from memory.

Is 3000 words enough?

An average adult native English speaker has an active vocabulary of about 20,000 words. However it has been said that a vocabulary of just 3000 words provides coverage for around 95% of common texts.

Statistics

Social networks

The greater part of Twitter users have English as their first or the second language. We analyzed a great amount of random tweets to find the most widely used words among young people.
